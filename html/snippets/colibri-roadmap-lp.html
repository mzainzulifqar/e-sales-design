Step 1: Increase Clarity

In this step, you begin by pondering certain questions and writing down whatever comes to mind without worrying about getting it right or wordsmithing.

The questions you need to consider are:

  • What do you care or get passionate about?
  • What difference do you want to make?
  • In the future, what difference do you want people to say you made?
  • What are the lasting effects you desire of your efforts? And why?

You need to fill out a grid focusing on what you are for, the outcomes you desire, and why what you have identified is important to you.

This step is all about identifying your core values and passions, and understanding why they are important to you.
